Advice to students: Mentality, mentality, mentality!

Many students have the question: what should I do to prepare for the graduate study? Instead of a list of papers to read or any technical skills, I believe mentality (initiative, attitude, etc.) is the most important. With the right mentality, everything follows.

The following article contains an excellent summary of advice for students (and even profs) to prepare their mentality for grad school. I just wish everyone could read it so that he/she doesn't have to learn the hard way.

Jason Hong. PhDs from the Faculty's Perspective, Blog@CACM, 2012.
